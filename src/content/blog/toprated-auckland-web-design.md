Kiwi Web Design has officially launched **TopRated.nz**, a new Auckland-focused business directory designed to make it easier for people to find trusted local service businesses online.


This is not a client project. TopRated.nz is an in-house platform built and owned by **Kiwi Web Design** to showcase how modern web design, SEO, and AI-friendly site architecture work together in real-world conditions.


You can view the new platform at **https://www.toprated.nz**.


### Why We Built TopRated.nz


Search behaviour is changing. People are no longer just typing simple keywords into Google. They are asking longer questions, using AI tools, and expecting clear, structured answers.


Most business directories have not adapted to this shift. They are cluttered with ads, thin listings, and pages that are hard for both users and AI systems to understand.


TopRated.nz was built to solve that problem by focusing on:


- Clear service-based categories


- Structured, easy-to-interpret content


- Strong location relevance for Auckland searches


- Clean page layouts with minimal distractions


The aim is simple: help users quickly identify top-rated service businesses, while giving those businesses a platform that supports real online visibility.


### Built With SEO and AI Discovery in Mind


As an [Auckland web design agency](/), we design websites for how people search today, not how they searched five years ago.


TopRated.nz is structured to support both traditional SEO and emerging AI-driven search experiences. Pages are written clearly, organised logically, and built around real search intent, such as users looking for “top rated” services rather than generic directories.


This approach helps ensure that businesses listed on the platform are discoverable not only through Google, but also through AI-powered tools and large language models that rely on context and entity-based information.


### Focused on Auckland Service Businesses


At launch, TopRated.nz focuses exclusively on service-based businesses in Auckland. This allows us to keep listings relevant and useful rather than spreading attention too thin.


Over time, the platform will expand into other New Zealand cities, following the same city-by-city approach to maintain quality and search relevance.
