In 2025, having only a Facebook page or relying on word of mouth isn’t enough for small businesses in Auckland. Customers expect professionalism, convenience, and the ability to find you on Google. At **Kiwi Web Design**, we’ve worked with countless small businesses across Auckland, and the difference a proper website makes is huge. Here are five reasons why a website is now essential.


### 1️⃣ Show Up on Google


Most customers begin their search online. If someone types “plumber in Auckland” or “best café in Mt Albert,” your business won’t appear without a proper website. A website gives you the chance to show up in Google searches and attract new customers daily.


### 2️⃣ Build Credibility and Trust


First impressions matter. A professional, modern website reassures potential customers that your business is reliable and established. Compared to a Facebook-only presence, a website signals that you’re serious and ready for business.


### 3️⃣ Be Available 24/7


A website works around the clock, even when you’re not. Customers can browse your services, read reviews, or contact you at any time-making your business accessible 24/7.


### 4️⃣ Control Your Online Presence


When you rely only on Facebook or social media, you’re at the mercy of their rules and algorithms. With a website, you own your space online. You decide how it looks, what it says, and how customers interact with you.
