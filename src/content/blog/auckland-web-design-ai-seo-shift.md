### SEO vs AEO: What’s the Difference?


| SEO | AEO |
| Keyword-focused | Question-focused |
| Optimised for Google bots | Optimised for AI agents |
| Ranks on search results | Delivers direct answers |
| Emphasises backlinks | Emphasises structure and schema |


AEO stands for **Answer Engine Optimisation**. It helps your website appear as a direct answer when users ask AI tools things like:*“Who builds affordable websites in Auckland?”*
