## FAQ: AI SEO, AEO, and GEO




What is the difference between SEO and AEO?

Traditional SEO aims to rank pages for keywords and earn clicks. AEO (Answer Engine Optimisation) focuses on structuring content so AI systems can extract and present direct answers, which can drive higher intent enquiries even with fewer clicks.




Does traditional SEO still matter in 2026?

Yes. Technical SEO, crawlability, and strong on-page fundamentals still matter. The change is that they are now baseline requirements. To win visibility, you also need structured, answer-ready content and stronger trust signals.




How do AI platforms choose which businesses to mention?

They tend to prefer sources that are clear, consistent, and credible. That includes strong topical depth, entity clarity, consistent business information (NAP), reviews, citations, and content that answers questions without fluff.




What should an Auckland small business do first?

Start with your core service pages. Make them specific, structured, and locally grounded. Add FAQs, comparison blocks, and schema. Then align your Google Business Profile and citations so your brand signals are consistent everywhere.




How long does it take to see results from AI SEO?

It depends on competition and how much authority already exists. Typically, you can improve clarity and conversion quickly, while visibility gains compound over time as content depth and trust signals strengthen.




Can you combine AI SEO with Google Ads?

Yes, and it’s often the fastest path to stable enquiries. Google Ads can provide controlled lead flow while AI SEO builds long-term visibility and lowers acquisition cost over time.
